27th of April 2023 - REDACTED

Prior to the finalisation of Warpath Jungle Vol I, we had this date with Young Gho$t and Unwell. Due to issues with mixed feelings from the DJs on the inclusion of the IRA fighters, as well as the war movie imagery, we decided to change the design concept entirely. Evolving more into what we have today. Due to a double booking at Valhalla, with the other show starting promotion earlier to us, the date ended up being changed to the 25th of May.

25th of May 2023 - Volume I

Originally an idea formed from the mind of Big Ferg L.H, Warpath had its humble begginings as a Wednesday night jungle night at Valhalla. Having spent the months leading up to Vol I working underneath Arekatera putting up posters, Ferg came up with the idea to throw a Hardcore Jungle night for Wellington. The collaboration between Big Ferg L.H and Arekatera happened quite naturally, with both DJs constantly exposing eachother to eachothers music inside of Arekateras 94 Corolla, with Arekatera quickly prooving to Ferg how valuable of a collaboration this would be with their combined knowledge. the collaboration naturally happened upon both DJs exposing eachother to various forms of Jungle that they were in love with, but felt were under represented in the Wellington music scene.
