Hall of Valour

Big Ferg L.H

Big Ferg L.H specializes in the deep, dark, and militant sounds of jungle and drum and bass. Spinning tracks from legends like Rumbleton and Friske, Ferg's sets are intense voyages that get hearts racing, spark anxiety, and fill you with the vigor needed to head into battle. A local legend in New Zealand's hardcore scene, Ferg has roots in bands like Severed Beliefs and has been instrumental in pushing AA events to keep the culture alive with the Start Today Crew.

Shampez

Hailing from the vibrant heart of the UK's original hardcore, jungle, and UK garage scenes, Shampez brings an unmatched energy and authenticity to every set. With "Original Junglist" proudly tattooed on his chest, Shampez is a living embodiment of the culture and passion that shaped these genres. A vinyl-only DJ, he seamlessly blends the raw sounds of jungle, hardcore, and garage, transporting audiences back to the golden era of underground music.
